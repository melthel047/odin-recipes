Goulash

Description

Goulash is a meal made of meat and vegetables seasoned with paprika and other spices.
Originating in Hungary, goulash is a common meal predominantly eaten in Central Europe
but also in other parts of Europe.
It is one the national dishes of Hungary and symbol of the country.

Ingredients

  • Paprika
  • Caraway seeds
  • Bay leaf
  • Butter
  • Beef chuck
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Beef stock/Broth
  • Potato & Carrot
  • Capsicum/Bell peppers
  • Garlic & Onion
  • Parsley
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cut beef
  2. Cook onion
  3. Cook beef
  4. Add garlic
  5. Spices
  6. Simmer
  7. Slow cook
