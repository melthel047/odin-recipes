Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Description

Shawarma is a popular dish originating from the Middle East.
It's a popular street food that is traditionally made in the form of spiced meats that are stacked
on the vertical rotisserie with thin sclices shaved off as it rotates.

It was tradionally made with lamb, mutton and chicken though these days you see it with beef too.
It's commonly served in the form of wrap, though in some countries you see it served on plates with sides such as seasoned rice or fries, and salads.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Chicken
  • Fresh Lemon
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Spices
    • Coriander Cumin
    • Cardamom
    • Paprika
    • Cayenne
    • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Place spices, yogurt, fresh lemon, and olive oil together in a bowl.
  2. Mix all the spices, yogurt, and olive oil well together.
  3. Add chicken to the mixture.
  4. Heat skillet on medium-high and place chicken.
  5. Grill chicken on both sides.
  6. Slice chicken.
